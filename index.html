    About Aaron Gerston

    I’m a data scientist with a passion for making a difference.

    Armed with an MSc in Biomedical Engineering from Denmark and a broad academic background in biochemistry, psychology, and computer science from the US, I excel at connecting the dots between complex problems and their optimal solutions.

    My journey has been fueled by a dedication to improving mental health diagnostics, and lately, I’ve been channeling that same energy into a passion for politics. From medical signal processing to natural language processing and all the data science in between, I bring a comprehensive toolkit and a genuine curiosity to every challenge, ensuring that data doesn’t just speak — it inspires action.
